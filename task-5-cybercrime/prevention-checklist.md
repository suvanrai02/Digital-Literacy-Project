# 🛡️ Stay Safe Online — Prevention Checklist
## For College Students in India

---

### 💳 Financial & UPI Safety
1. ✅ Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone — 
   not even bank officials
2. ✅ Remember — you NEVER need to enter your PIN 
   to RECEIVE money, only to SEND

### 🔐 Account & Password Safety
3. ✅ Use strong passwords with a mix of letters, 
   numbers and symbols
4. ✅ Never use the same password for multiple accounts
5. ✅ Enable two factor authentication on all important 
   accounts

### 📱 Social Media Safety
6. ✅ Never accept friend requests from unknown people
7. ✅ Keep your personal information private on 
   all platforms
8. ✅ Never click on suspicious links received via 
   WhatsApp or SMS

### 🌐 General Online Safety
9. ✅ Always verify the website URL before making 
   any payment
10. ✅ Never use public Wi-Fi for banking or 
    UPI transactions

---

### 🚨 Report Cybercrime Immediately
- 🌐 National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
- 📞 Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (24x7)
